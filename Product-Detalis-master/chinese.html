To make Chinese vegetable rice, you start by selecting fresh vegetables like carrots, bell peppers, broccoli, mushrooms, and snow peas. These veggies are chopped into bite-sized pieces and stir-fried in a wok with aromatic seasonings such as garlic, ginger, and soy sauce. Next, fragrant jasmine or long-grain rice is cooked separately until fluffy and tender. Once cooked, the rice is combined with the stir-fried vegetables, creating a vibrant and nutritious dish.
