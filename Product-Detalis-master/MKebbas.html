Mutton kebabs are a delicious dish enjoyed by many. They're made with tender mutton meat seasoned with spices and then cooked on skewers. First, we start with the mutton. Mutton is a type of meat from sheep that's rich in flavor. We cut it into small pieces and then season it with spices like salt, pepper, and cumin. This gives the meat lots of taste. Next, we shape the seasoned mutton into long, thin cylinders and put them onto skewers. This makes it easier to cook and eat. Now, we cook the kebabs. We can grill them over hot coals, roast them in the oven, or even cook them on a stovetop grill. As they cook, the spices mix with the meat, creating a mouthwatering aroma. Once the kebabs are cooked through and have a nice brown color on the outside, they're ready to eat. Some people like to squeeze lemon juice over them or sprinkle them with fresh herbs like mint or cilantro for extra flavor. Mutton kebabs are often served with a side of yogurt sauce called raita and some warm flatbread like naan or pita. They're perfect for a barbecue with friends or a cozy dinner at Restaurant.
